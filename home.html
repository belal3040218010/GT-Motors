What defines a GT?

The concept of the GT car emerged from Europe in the mid-1900s,
with the goal of being both a high-performance automobile and a long-distance driver.
So GT — meaning grand tourer or gran tourer — makes sense. Some hallmarks of
GT models include a near-seamless merging of luxury and performance and
a 2+2 design
